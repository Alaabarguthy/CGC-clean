Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Artelco ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of its employees. This Privacy Policy explains how the Artelco Employee App collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Authentication Data: We collect your professional email and password provided by Artelco to authenticate your access to the internal ERP system.

Professional Data: The app accesses and displays your professional details stored in our internal ERP, including your name, employee code, assigned tasks, and overtime records.

Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device (model, OS version) to ensure app compatibility and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core app functionality (Accessing tasks, managing overtime).
  • To synchronize approved overtime with our internal Menaitech HR system.
  • To send notifications regarding task updates or overtime status via Telegram.
  • To maintain and improve the security of our internal systems.

3. Data Storage and Security

The Artelco Employee App is an internal tool. Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to Artelco's private servers. We do not sell or share employee data with third-party advertisers or external marketing agencies.

4. Third-Party Services

Menaitech: Approved overtime data is synchronized with the Menaitech HRMS system as per Artelco's HR policies.

Telegram: We use Telegram's API to send automated professional notifications to technical staff.

5. Data Retention

Professional data is retained in accordance with Artelco's standard corporate data retention policies.
